Art

This section contains my artistic work, including images and movies, in publications, exhibits, performances, and installations. All my images with linear fractals are collected here. Please follow the links below to view details.

Exhibits, Performances, and Installations

  • The Bus Project, with K. Irwin et al.: an interactive, interdisciplinary installation exhibited at the STC Bus Depots in Regina and Saskatoon in May/June 2004 as part of SPASM II: The Couture of Contemporaneity, a festival of public art works organized by PAVED media arts centre and AKA Gallery.
  • Triangularhythmic (2004), by David Gerhard, Daryl H. Hepting, and Matthew McKague, shown as part of the Interactive Digital Jukebox at the International Computer Music Conference (ICMC) 2004.
  • 10th Annual SFU Faculty, Staff, and Student exhibition, November 1996.
  • Electronic Arts Festival, Graphicon '96: 6th International Conference and Exhibition on Computer Graphics and Visualization in Russia, Saint Petersburg, Russia, July 1-5, 1996.
  • MATH-ART. Exhibit at the Teck Gallery, Simon Fraser University Harbour Centre Campus. 9 December 1995 -- 24 January 1996. Organized by E. Gibson, M. Dubiel, K. Heinrich, and D. Hepting.
  • Algorithmic Art Show, Xerox PARC, Palo Alto, California. June 15 -- August 12, 1994. Organized by M. Bern.
  • Exploring the Image World of Computer Graphics. Exhibit at W.A.C. Bennett Library, Simon Fraser University. May -- July, 1994. Organized by D. H. Hepting.
  • New York Notes: Music and Fractals by B. B. Mandelbrot and Charles Wuorinen, debuted at the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum, April 19, 1990.
