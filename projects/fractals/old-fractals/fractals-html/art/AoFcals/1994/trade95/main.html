The 1994/95 Calendar

The 1994 Fractals/1995 The Art of Fractals calendar was edited by F. Kenton Musgrave, and published by Universe. It features an essay by Musgrave and contributions from John C. Hart, Daryl H. Hepting, Henry Kaufman, Craig Kolb, Przemyslaw Prusinkiewicz, Dietmar Saupe, Karl Sims, and Kevin Suffern.

You can browse the calendar month by month [avg. 34Kb], or load all 12 months [124Kb] at once.

If you have any questions about this calendar, please send mail to musgrave@seas.gwu.edu
