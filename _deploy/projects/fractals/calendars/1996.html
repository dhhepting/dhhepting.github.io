1996

I edited the 1996 Fractals calendar as a Ph.D. student at Simon Fraser University.
The calendar featured images from: Robby Alyn Berry, Brian Evans, Michael Field, Martin Golubitsky, Daryl H. Hepting, F. Kenton Musgrave, Kevin Suffern, and Eric Tosan. Thanks also to: Joelle Thollot.

This calendar was completed with the assistance of the Centre for Experimental and Constructive Mathematics and the Graphics and Multimedia Research Laboratory at Simon Fraser University. Earlier work was done at the University of Regina and at the IBM Thomas J. Watson Research Center.

There are many to whom I continue to be grateful, but especially B. Mandelbrot, K. Musgrave, P. Prusinkiewicz, D. Saupe, and R. Voss.
