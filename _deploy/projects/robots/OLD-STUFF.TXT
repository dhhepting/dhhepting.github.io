		<h3 id="#tourney">Tournament</h3>
		<p>
			The tournament is an integral part of the FIRST LEGO League season.
			In Saskatchewan, our provincial championship tournament will be held on a Saturday
			in January.  You can share your preferences by <a href="http://doodle.com/poll/5tksetrf6gr238ic">completing this doodle poll</a>.  After creating your FIRST account and signing in:
			<ul>
				<li>All team coaches can see the tournament.  Please participate!</li>
				<li>All volunteers can select this event.</li>
			</ul>
