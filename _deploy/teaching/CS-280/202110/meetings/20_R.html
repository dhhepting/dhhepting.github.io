  • CIPS (Canadian Information Processing Society) is a federation which provides many services to IT professionals in Canada. They provide certifications to IT professionals: the AITP, ISP, and ITCP designations. These certifications tell employers that the certified member follows a code of ethics and has a high level of knowledge and ability. They also provide accreditation of university programs which allows students from accredited universities to have a fast track to ISP and ITCP designations. Lastly, they provide a job board for IT professionals to find work.
