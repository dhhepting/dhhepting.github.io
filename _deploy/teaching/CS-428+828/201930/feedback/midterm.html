Midterm

This page presents data obtained from my own feedback instrument that I give to students in my classes at their midterm and final exams. I look at midterm feedback when it is received because I consider it to be a formative evaluation of the class in progress. I do not see the final feedback until after the final grades are posted. The numbers that precede each response are present to permit matching between the data table (click button to toggle expanded view) and the opened ended questions. A response with a — or NA indicates that no answer was given. Open-ended question responses have been transcribed as faithfully as possible, with minor edits and formatting changes sometimes required.
